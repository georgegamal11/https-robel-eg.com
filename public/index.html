FAQ

What is the unit delivery date?
There are immediate delivery units, and units under construction are delivered by December 2028.
Are cash discounts available?
Yes, we provide discounts up to 25% when paying cash during special offer periods.
What are the maintenance fees?
Maintenance fees are about 10% of the unit value and are paid upon receipt to ensure service quality and project beauty.
How can I book a visit?
You can fill out the form below or call the sales line directly to coordinate a free visit to the project on site.
